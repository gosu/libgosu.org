By jlnr (dev) Date 2009-06-21 02:18 Edited 2009-06-21 02:23
You always need to use Xcode and a fresh build of Gosu from SVN, and I can't offer real support for the iPhone version beyond that yet. It's kind of the adventure port of Gosu where you have the information posted in the other iPhone/C++ thread, and are expected to know your way around Xcode for general iPhone development to make the switch to Gosu. Most of the setup work is outside the scope of this board.

But yes, sound and music work, except looping of songs and MID/S3M/XM/IT/MOD files. You can guess some of this stuff by tracking the ToDo wiki page.
