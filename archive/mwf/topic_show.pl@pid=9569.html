It appears that active Gosu conversations are now taking place primarily on Discord.

The ||#introduce yourself|| tab is a nice place to get started there. However, the Discord channel seems a bit congested. Threading is a theoretical possibility, but most replies seem to get posted as a new comment, not a response, with about 80% of conversations appearing in the ||#general|| tab. I am searching for more of a ||#help|| tab, where I can post my silly newb questions, so I don't muddy up the ||#general|| discussion.

I wonder if it might be helpful to spread things out a little bit, by adding one or two extra tabs in the Discord channel. This way, people could have an easier time going back and finding a given conversation, because it would be spread out a little more, instead of all being squished into one scrollable column.

With regard to backing up the old forum: My personal experience with website data loss tells me that it is actually the DATABASE, and not the website implementation, which needs to get backed up.

Best wishes to all!
