Just wanted to collect the four entries written in Gosu for the last Ludum Dare gamedev compo, I hope noone minds me just posting them! :) They all have Win32 builds, some have Mac builds too, and source is available for all of them.

HybridMind's 2D oldschool rampage game CoMuTor (Ruby): http://www.ludumdare.com/compo/tag/comutor/

mathias wrote a challenging C++ puzzler: http://www.ludumdare.com/compo/2008/12/07/crazy-taxi-puzzling-game-final/

flgr's philosophical arcade game, with a very interesting map generator (Ruby): http://www.ludumdare.com/compo/2008/12/07/doctortease-final/

My own not-too-challenging Ruby puzzler: http://www.ludumdare.com/compo/2008/12/07/puzzle-road-final/
