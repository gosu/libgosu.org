Written by Team Tortilla (Florian Groß, Alexander Post, Julian Raschke) for the 72h competition in the summer of 2006, this is a puzzle game with unusual controls, some nice touches like parallax scrolling and a particle engine. It could be a bit more intuitive and longer, admittedly ;)
