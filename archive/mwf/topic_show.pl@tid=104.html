By DKnight Date 2009-06-17 15:44
I know this is old, but I had the same problem with the latest gosu, ruby-opengl, and ruby releases (both 1.8 and 1.9)
After looking around a bit, I found a solution. Just issue the following gl command when initializing your game window;

Gl.disable_error_checking

The demo runs perfect thereafter.
