By jlnr (dev) Date 2009-05-17 15:45
Shinobi Chef, using more SVG files is often discussed. You could either use some build scripts to convert SVG into pixel graphics before shipping your game, or you could do it on startup (depending on screen resolution, for example). RMagick should be able to do this, even though people have reported problems with the alpha channel and SVG graphics.
If you don't want to rasterize the images ever, you can build a pure-Ruby library on top of Gosu's draw_line, draw_triangle and draw_quad primitives. So in any case, the possibility is there, someone just needs to write it, and in the case of RMagick, solve the deployment woes that still arise on Windows.
