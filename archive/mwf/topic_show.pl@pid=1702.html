By ippa Date 2009-11-10 22:31
gosu needs the fmod.dll for sound, it comes with the gem.
Make sure you bundle fmod.dll in the exe with ocra.. you can put it in lib\ for example:

C:\>ocra main.rb --windows lib\**

Also try this in the top of your main.rb:

GAMEROOT = File.dirname(File.expand_path($0))
$: << File.join(GAMEROOT,"lib")

Hey.. did you check out the new Ocra-gem? works good with XP and 1.9.1 now.. anyhow, I recently made an exe of ghost (my newest game). This is the relevant files:

http://github.com/ippa/ghost/blob/master/start_game.rb

require 'chingu'
$: << File.join(ROOT,"lib")
ENV['PATH'] = File.join(ROOT,"lib") + ";" + ENV['PATH']

Also you need fmod.dll in ROOT\lib\  ! (Ocra detects and includes every dll / gem in Ghost but fmod.dll for some reason)
