Topic Gosu / Gosu Showcase / Shapeshifter.
By Basic Date 2012-05-03 01:19 Edited 2012-05-03 01:30
This is a group project (7 in total) for a Uni module.

You play as a paper guy who can fold himself into other objects to move around the world.
Made for iPad, Dev'd on Windows, the only porting issues came from the zip library.

The art style for background and foreground is rather odd, this is because the two artists, didn't quite meet in the middle as planned, when doing their artwork.
The images shown are running on the iPhone (I think they are a little blurry because we aren't using the Retinia images, looks much better on the actual phone), all the scaling is off because the target platform is the iPad, and I don't have one atm :)
