Topic Gosu / Gosu Showcase / Inception (2d top down survival shooter)
By Julian Sansat Date 2015-01-28 01:18
Hi guys, I'm from Brazil and this game was made for a school project and then in the global game jam 2015 was remake and upgraded, the game started as a zombie survival, but then was added more scenarios and different enemies and scenarios like mummies in the desert, aliens in the futuristic theme and demons in the hell scenario.
The history of the game is that the character is trapped in a nightmare and to wake up from that nightmare he need to beat his main dream which is about a zombie apocalypse, and the objective is to collect items for turn on the car and get away, the necessary items are the keys, gas and battery. The problem is that his nightmare has more layers and the items are spread over them and the dreams (scenarios) can change anytime.
The game has two weapons, a pistol and a shotgun, wich you can alternate picking up from the ground.
