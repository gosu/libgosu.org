I should definitely write a wiki-style page about transformations. (Contributions by those who managed to understand my sparse documentation are always welcome ;) )

  my_window.rotate(54) do
    # Everything in here will first be rotated by 54° degrees, then moved left by 200 pixels
    # Use this for scrolling, or for implementing composite objects in an easier way
    image.draw...
    my_window.draw_quad...
  end
end
