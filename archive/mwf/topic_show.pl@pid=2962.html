By jlnr (dev) Date 2010-08-03 23:59
You need 3D hardware acceleration, which every machine should have by now.

After that, it's simply a matter of how much computation your game needs. Unlike other language/toolkit combinations, Ruby/Gosu performance starts off pretty good, but can come to a halt quite quickly too. Try to keep your game logic simple. (Good that you are not a previous Java programmer.)

(This applies to Windows and OS X, Linux seems less predictable to me.)
